Your hands. Your head.
A little relief.

Guided head massage routines for people whose heads carry the weight of the day — and for anyone who knows what it's like when the light gets to be too much.

Head Rub was built by a migraine sufferer who spent years looking for something that fitted into her life and didn't require an appointment. She's a product designer. Her head hurts a lot.

The routines are narrated by her father — a school custodian from Ohio with a naturally calm voice. Not a hired narrator. Not a synthesized assistant. A real person, chosen for love.

Head Rub is a wellness practice, not a substitute for medical care. If you experience severe or frequent head pain, please consult a healthcare provider.
